    Past Trustee's

    Dr.Prabha Khaitan is a President’s Award winning writer, Businesswoman and social activist. She is the Ex-President of the Calcutta Chamber of Commerce and has been acclaimed for her Hindi translation of Simone de Beauvoir’s “The Second Sex”. She also founded the Prabha Khaitan Foundation, dedicated to the social, cultural and educational advancement of society.

    Late Dr.Prabha Khaitan

    Late S.N.Bajaj
    Present Trustee's

    Mr.Bhutoria is a social activist involved with a wide range of charitable activities, including the Prabha Khaitan Foundation, Rajasthan National Forum and the International Marwari Federation Youth Wing and also the World Federation of United Nations Association. He has represented India at a number of international forums on youth affairs.

    Sundeep Bhutoria

    Mr. Bangur is the Managing Director of Shree Cement, a leading name in the cement industry. He has served as a key functionary in trade bodies such as the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry and is also an active Rotarian. Mr. Bangur has also actively been associated with the philanthropic endeavours of the Bangur Group. Education For All Trust was formed by Dr. Prabha Khaitan,S.N.Bajaj and Sundeep Bhutoria while H.M.Bangur joined later as a trustee.

    H.M. Bangur

    Manjari Bhutoria believes that the first step to having anything in life is education, and primary education is the first step which should be available to all children. Being an MBA in finance herself she feels that one can go far if one has the right direction. For her, this began from school where she took part in various activities to help underprivileged children. She also adds "it’s very important to ensure primary education to children if we want a better future and it is the right of every child to be educated for a better world".
